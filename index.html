OUR INSTRUCTORS

Monitor Guillermo Vidal, aka “Mandrake”, has been leading Capoeira Brasil Cincinnati since 2018. He began training capoeira in 2010, developing a style rooted in discipline, creativity, and the rich traditions of the art.

A self‑described fitness junkie, Mandrake brings high energy and physical intensity to his classes, inspiring students to push their limits while staying connected to the cultural and musical foundations of capoeira. His teaching blends strong fundamentals with a welcoming, community‑driven approach.

Beyond the group, Guillermo is a major figure in Cincinnati’s Latin American community, helping organize cultural events that celebrate heritage, unity, and diversity. With family roots in Belo Horizonte and Minas Gerais, he carries the spirit of Brazil into every roda, workshop, and community gathering.

Professor Anthony “Santo” Domingo is the Head Instructor of Capoeira Brasil Ohio (CBOH) and oversees programs in Cleveland, Columbus, and Cincinnati. With over 20 years of Capoeira experience, Santo has built high-quality programs that teach technique, tradition, and the value of community to students of all ages. Capoeira Brasil Cincinnati is part of this network, led locally by Monitor Mandrake under Santo’s guidance.

Santo is also a Black Belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and the Owner & Head Instructor at VIA MARTIAL ARTS in Solon OH, where he continues to teach and mentor students in both Capoeira and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. His training philosophy focuses on safety, technical proficiency, and a solid work ethic.

OUR CORE VALUES & PHILOSOPHY

At Capoeira Brasil Columbus our core values & philosophy are focused on the fact that capoeira is for everyone, community and family oriented, positive energy, passionate team, diversity, and acceptance.

+ - Capoeira is for EVERYONE

Capoeira is for everyone, regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, gender identity, age, sexual orientation, ability, physical appearance, and religion. We are an inclusive environment where everyone is welcome to learn, grow, and express themselves through capoeira.

+ - Encourages community and family

We believe capoeira is a lifestyle. Capoeira Brasil Columbus is an operated community that wants to bring the family sense to all our members.

+ - Ensures positivity and passion

We are focused on the positive energy to support the growth of each member of our group — from the little kids to the adults. CONNECT, MOVE, TRANSFORM.

+ - Promotes diversity and acceptance

One of our main focuses is to create an inclusive, nonjudgemental and stress-free space where everyone that comes to our class feels free to be 100% themselves.
